REVIEW Of the show The Vanpire Diaries (2009-2017)

Jankairy GR, January 29, 2024

★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

The Vanpire Diaries was a journey considering theres 171 episodes. I enjoyed watching the drama and the romance. The character development of each cast was great to see. i loved how each episode was unexpeting and had you on your feet. At times the back stories got confusing,the show added many details that would end up puzzling. The overall of the show was great and would recommend if you have lots of time on your hands. 💗 One thing i'll say is that i'm team Damon all the way.💗

Trivia About The Vanpire Diaries

  • 🤔All of the episodes titles for the last season were quotes from the first season.🤔
  • 🤑The three main characters got paid $40k each episode. 🤑
  • 😎Ian Somerhalder auditioned twice for the role "Damon Salvatore" until he got it. 😎
